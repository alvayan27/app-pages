Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Thank you for using StopStop Parental ("the App"). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other data that could identify you personally.

2. Children's Screen Time Data

The App uses Apple's Screen Time API to help parents manage children's device usage. All related data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

3. Subscription Information

Subscription payments are processed through the Apple App Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment information.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. All data is stored locally on your device and does not pass through our servers.

The App does not use any third-party analytics tools or advertising services.
