常见问题

Q：如何设置屏幕时间限制？

打开 StopStop Parental，选择需要管理的设备，点击"设置时间限制"，按照引导完成配置即可。

Q：订阅如何取消？

前往 iPhone「设置」→ 点击您的名字 →「订阅」→ 找到 StopStop Parental → 点击「取消订阅」。

Q：支持哪些设备？

本应用支持运行 iOS 16.0 及以上系统的 iPhone 和 iPad。

Q：数据会上传到云端吗？

不会。所有数据均存储在您的本地设备上，不会上传至任何服务器。

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I set screen time limits?

Open StopStop Parental, select the device you want to manage, tap "Set Time Limit", and follow the on-screen instructions.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → find StopStop Parental → tap Cancel Subscription.

Q: Which devices are supported?

The app supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 16.0 or later.

Q: Is my data uploaded to the cloud?

No. All data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server.
